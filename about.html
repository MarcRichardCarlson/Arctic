Carbon neutral

An in-depth assessment of the carbon footprint of our wider business operations, and also a strategy to reduce up and downstream greenhouse gas emissions, will take time to implement. We will work on this in close cooperation with our suppliers and experts. In partnership with Forestmatic, we’re planting a new tree in the tropics in Uganda with every single online and offline order, so as to fully offset the carbon footprint of every product that we sell.
